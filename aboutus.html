Our Team at Dale's Cafe

Here at Dale's, we source sustainably to bring our food to your plate for years to come. We know the importance of bringing the best to you, our customers, so we only contract with suppliers who practice sustainable techniques in production of our ingredients.
Our Business model is simple: our customers help choose the menu! We take our reviews seriously, and we periodically update the menu to reflect what our guests are saying - we'll even add new items if customers suggest it!
So, take an evening and try our customer-backed dishes and you'll see what makes us, special. Be sure to check out our website, make an account, and leave a review afterwards. We'll see you soon.
